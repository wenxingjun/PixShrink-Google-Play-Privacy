This Privacy Policy explains how PixShrink (“the App”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App. We respect your privacy and handle data in accordance with Google Play policies and applicable laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide or Authorize

  • Photos and Image Files (local) – Used to compress, preview, and save results.
  • Image Metadata (local) – Such as creation date, camera model, or GPS data (if present). You may choose to remove certain metadata during compression.
  • App Settings – Compression quality, export format, metadata removal preferences, etc., stored locally for user experience.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and system information (e.g., device model, OS version)
  • Usage data and crash logs for analytics and stability improvement

2. How We Use Information

  • Perform photo compression and export
  • Generate previews and comparisons
  • Store local history or cache
  • Support settings and metadata removal
  • Show ads or manage subscriptions
  • Improve stability and performance (analytics/crash reporting)

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Photos and compressed outputs are stored locally on your device by default.
  • History/cache is stored in the App’s private storage.
  • You can delete history or clear cache inside the App.
  • Uninstalling the App removes its local data.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers, such as:

  • Google Play Billing (subscriptions)
  • Ad networks (for ads)
  • Analytics / Crash reporting services

5. Android Permissions

  • Read Media/Photos – Required to select and compress images (`READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` / `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`).
  • Write Media (if saving to gallery) – Needed to save compressed images to the device gallery.
  • Network Access – Required for ads, subscriptions, and analytics.

6. Ads and Subscriptions

  • Free users may see ads.
  • Subscriptions are managed by Google Play; we do not process payment details directly.
  • Ad providers may use device identifiers for ad delivery or analytics.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is discovered, please contact us to remove it.

8. Your Choices

  • Deny photo permission (some features will not work)
  • Clear app cache/history
  • Uninstall the App to remove local data

For data-related requests, contact: wenxingjun@gmai.com.

9. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of changes.
